How to Use This Calculator

The Down Payment Savings Calculator projects exactly how long it will take to reach your home purchasing goals. To construct your savings timeline, input the target home purchase price and your preferred down payment target percentage. Enter your starting savings balance and the amount you can comfortably contribute to your savings fund each month. Finally, input the expected annual investment or savings interest return. The calculator instantly evaluates your inputs, projecting the exact number of months needed to reach standard key down payment thresholds (3%, 5%, 10%, and 20%). A gorgeous cumulative savings curve chart visualizes your milestones over time, while our Plain-English summaries help you decide if you need to adjust your target home price or accelerate your savings rate.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to save for a down payment? +
It depends on your target home price, starting balance, and monthly savings rate. This calculator maps out the exact months required for key targets.
What is the minimum down payment for a first-time homebuyer? +
Conventional loans allow down payments as low as 3%, FHA loans allow 3.5%, while USDA and VA loans allow 0% down for qualifying properties and borrowers.
Can I save my down payment in a high-yield savings account? +
Yes! Storing your down payment savings in a high-yield savings account (HYSA) or low-risk investment is highly recommended to earn competitive returns safely.
