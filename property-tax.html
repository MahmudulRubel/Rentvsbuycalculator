How to Use This Calculator

The Property Tax Calculator estimates your state-level tax liability and ranks property taxes across the nation. To see your estimate, select your US state from our dropdown menu to auto-configure regional effective tax rate averages, and input your home purchase price. The calculator instantly estimates your total annual property tax, your monthly escrow tax obligation, and your state's rank relative to the rest of the country. An active bar chart displays your estimated tax against national minimums and maximums, while a full, sortable tax table displays the effective tax rates for all 50 states, letting you compare regional cost liabilities perfectly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How are property taxes calculated? +
Property taxes are calculated by multiplying the assessed market value of your property by your local municipal and county tax rates.
Which state has the lowest property tax rate? +
Hawaii historically features the lowest effective state-level property tax rate at approximately 0.29%, whereas New Jersey features the highest at over 2.4%.
How do property taxes affect monthly mortgage payments? +
Lenders collect 1/12th of your annual property tax each month in an escrow account, directly raising your monthly out-of-pocket housing expenses.
