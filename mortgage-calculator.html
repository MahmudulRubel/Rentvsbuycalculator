How to Use This Calculator

This Mortgage Payment Calculator helps you estimate your total monthly housing expenses with rigorous precision. To use this calculator, start by inputting the total list price of the property you intend to buy, followed by your planned down payment. The interest rate should be set to the annual interest percentage offered by your lender, and the loan term can be set to 15, 20, or 30 years using the buttons. You can also customize property taxes, homeowners insurance, and monthly HOA fees. The results panel will instantly compute your total monthly mortgage payment, displaying an interactive Pie Chart to visualize the exact proportions of principal, interest, taxes, insurance, and HOA fees. Scroll down to review the fully detailed amortization table breakdown, illustrating exactly how your remaining loan balance decreases with every single monthly payment over the life of the loan.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this mortgage calculator free? +
Yes! Our Mortgage Payment Calculator is 100% free with no signups, credit cards, or accounts required.
What does a monthly mortgage payment include? +
A typical monthly mortgage payment includes principal repayment, loan interest fees, local property taxes, homeowner's insurance, and monthly HOA fees where applicable.
Can I pay off my mortgage early to save money? +
Absolutely! Making extra principal payments can dramatically reduce the length of your mortgage and save you thousands of dollars in total interest.
