How to Use This Calculator

This Rent vs Buy Calculator helps you compare the long-term financial impact of renting a home versus purchasing a property. To get started, simply input your current or projected monthly rent on the left side of the tool, followed by the home purchase price you are targeting. You can fine-tune your scenario using the down payment inputs, which conveniently synchronize between a cash amount and a percentage value. Don't forget to customize the mortgage interest rate, annual home appreciation rate, rent increase rate, property taxes, home insurance, and monthly HOA fees. As you adjust the slider representing your planned years to stay, the calculator instantly re-amortizes the figures and produces a dynamic financial comparison. The results panel will clearly show you which option saves you more money over your selected time horizon, complete with a visual break-even chart and a detailed year-by-year amortization breakdown.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this rent vs buy calculator free to use? +
Yes! Our calculator is 100% free with no email registration, credit card details, or hidden accounts required.
How accurate is the rent vs buy calculator? +
Our calculator utilizes full-amortization schedules, property appreciation modeling, state/local tax estimations, and lost opportunity cost formulations, providing highly rigorous estimates equivalent to commercial underwriting systems.
What is a break-even point in rent vs buy? +
The break-even point is the specific year where the net value of homeownership (accrued home equity minus all transactional and monthly buying expenses) surpasses the net cash of renting and investing the down payment elsewhere.
